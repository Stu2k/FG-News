BREAKING: Newham Bans Cars, Cats, and Cutlery

By East End Observer & Occasional Sausage Roll Reviewer, Stuart “Woke-but-Wary” Templeton

🚗 Car-Free Newham Initiative

The car ban came into effect at 5am this morning. Every vehicle was towed by bicycles pulling reinforced oat-milk-powered trailers. “It’s honestly a spiritual cleanse,” said Ash Wildroot, a 27-year-old barista/candlemaker. “Now I can skateboard to my foraging circles without inhaling diesel.”

LTNs will now be enforced by emotionally sensitive drones that deploy haikus and biodegradable glitter bombs. "The LTNs will save all,” reads the borough mural.

🐱 Total Cat Ban Announced

Domestic cats have been banned after “rising tensions with foxes.” A Cat Amnesty Bin will be installed outside Stratford Centre. Residents are asked to deposit cats with a poem, softly and lovingly, by Thursday.

🦊 Foxes Are the New Pets

Foxes have been reclassified as companion animals. Recommended names: Gary, Snuffles, or Michael Gove. How to tame:

  • Leave a trail of Richmond sausages
  • Offer a name
  • Do not attempt jumpers. You will lose a toe.

🔪 Knife Ban in Forest Gate, Spoons Rise

All knives are banned. “Spoons are round, peaceful, and emotionally safe,” says Councillor Donna Pease-Breeze. A borough-wide spoon training course starts next week. Chopsticks remain under investigation.

🔥 Chaos on Wanstead Flats

Two men — See You and Next Tuesday — fled a BBQ fire after a missing cat triggered a full fire engine response. They were stopped by The Hunter, a mysterious figure in wellies and a compostable balaclava.

🪜 Laddermania 2025 & The Forest Gate Dads

Shoutout to the FG Dads with Ladders group for incredible ladder reviews and their new slogan: “LADDERS UP, NEWHAM!” Special thanks to Colin T. for the Werner Fibreglass and Steve B. for wise hinge-based trust advice. A statue is being built near Upton Park KFC.
